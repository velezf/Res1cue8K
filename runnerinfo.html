Res1cue Run 8K and Kids Ambulance Chase Runner Information

Race day is May 4, 2014 at 8:00AM

Thank you to all our sponsors and runners!

Runner Information

8K Race Details

Date: Sunday May 04, 2014
Race Start: 8:00 AM

Race Day Packet Pickup Begins at 7AM
Start Line: Bethesda-Chevy Chase Rescue Squad
Race Limit: 600
Restrictions: 1.5 hour time limit, Must complete course by 9:30AM

Port-a-johns will be provided at the start and finish.

Packet Pickup

Runners will be able to pick up their packets at the Bethesda Chevy Chase Rescue Squad starting on race day. Pick up is available anytime between 7:00AM and 7:50AM. Please pick up your packets early to ensure you are ready to run. A bag check station will be also be available.

The Course

The USATF-certified course starts and ends at Bethesda-Chevy Chase Rescue Squad at 5020 Battery Lane, Bethesda, MD. Runners will proceed onto Battery Lane then turn right onto Old Georgetown Rd.. Runners will continue down Old Georgetown Rd., past Suburban Hospital and the National Institutes of Health (NIH) and turn right onto Beech Ave. From there runners will make a slight left on to Linden Ave and then right onto Pooks Hill Rd.. Continuing downhill runners will make a right onto Rockville Pike and run up past NIH and Navy Medical Center. Runners will continue down Wisconsin Avenue, turning right onto Fairmont Avenue and then making a quick right onto Woodmont Ave. Runners reach the final stretch by turning left onto Battery Lane and finish in front of the Rescue Squad.

Runner Premium

All volunteers and participants will receive a custom designed synthetic performance shirt.

Race Awards

All finishers will receive the inaugural BCCRS Res1cue Run finishers medal.

About The Kids Ambulance Chase

The Kids Ambulance Chase is open to all children 12 and under. There are two age groups: 7 and under (Ambulance Division, approx 1/10 mile) and 8-12 (Medic Division, approx 3/10 mile). The two age groups will run different lengths with all participants crossing the same finish line. All participants will receive a T-shirt, water and Gatorade.

Register for the Kids Ambulance Chase and see a show by the Great Zucchini!

Washington's funniest and most magical PRESCHOOL & KINDERGARTEN entertainer, with over 12 years of experience making kids laugh!

WHEN: May 4, 2014 at 9:00AM. Shortly after Kids Fun runs.

 

About the Cut Above Challenge

We are thrilled to announce the "CUT ABOVE" fire/rescue competition -- a race within the Res1cue Run 8K!  Contest rules:   Each station, department, shift, or recruit class team may have as many runners as desired.  The top three finish times for each team will be totaled, for your team finish time. The team with the fastest team finish will be engraved on our "Cut Above Res1cue Run" trophy (featuring Hurst cutter blades) …and the winning team takes it home to their station until next year's event.

All fire/rescue/public safety personnel receive a 25% discount on registration

Parking

Parking will be available off site. Garages within easy walking distance on Del Ray, St. Elmo, and Woodmont. All Bethesda garage locations can be found at montgomerycountymd.gov under parking.

Safety Considerations

During the race runners will have one roadway lane open to the course. Vehicle traffic will be proceeding just beyond the cone lane. Please stay within the coned lanes. In the event emergency vehicles must cross the course, runners will be stopped briefly to allow passage. Please listen and take instruction from all course marshals and police officers on the course.

For safety and insurance reasons please adhere to the following on the course: no dogs, no headphones, no strollers, no bicycles, no roller blades, no in-line skates, no wheeled vehicles.

Timing and Scoring

We are pleased to announce Montgomery County Road Runners Club will be handling the Res1cue Run timing and scoring.

Please remember to remove your timing chip after finishing the race, and place it in the collection bins.

8K Results

Results will be posted as soon as they become available on this website.

At The Finish

Medals will be awarded once runners cross the finish line and return their timing chips.

Drinks and refreshments will be available, along with advanced medical teams.

 

 

Motorist Tips

Please drive with caution along the course. Roads will remain open with limited delays to allow runners to pass.

Old Georgetown Rd will remain open to traffic throughout the race.

355 will remain open to traffic throughout the race.

 

Spectator Tips

Spectators are encouraged along the entire course. Please be vigilant when crossing the street.

Spectators are encouraged to stay on the sidewalks and avoid encroachment on the course. Listen to all instructions given by course marshals.

Please observe all posted traffic restrictions when parking in neighborhoods.
