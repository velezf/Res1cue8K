Why Run With Us?

Supporting Your Local Rescue Squad

When you choose to run with us all proceeds from the event go to benefit the Bethesda-Chevy Chase Rescue Squad. This money stays in your community to serve you and your neighbors with state of the art emergency medical, fire, and rescue services. The Rescue Squad does not receive regular appropriated funds from any government. Instead, we raise almost all of our $2 million annual operating budget from individual donors, foundations, businesses, and occasional state and federal grants. The Rescue Squad is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. When you choose to run with us, your choosing to support your community heroes.

 

 

The Start, The Course, The Finish

The race begins at the Bethesda-Chevy Chase Rescue Squad. Here local volunteers have been answering the call for over 76 years. You can drop off your bag, soak up the atmosphere, and get ready to support your world class local rescue squad mile by mile.

The course will take you through the heart of the BCCRS response area. Past Suburban hospital, the closest trauma center, and though just a small fraction of the neighborhoods in the response area of the rescue squad.

You will finish at "R1" (what members call the station). Here you can celebrate, enjoy your achievement, and enjoy post race festivities.

 

Great Value

With a modest entry fee thanks to amazing sponsors, you get the course and organization of a large race with out the price tag.
